The Process

From Request to
Delivery in Minutes.

01

Request

Rural clinic submits an emergency payload request via our secure portal or USSD shortcode.

02

Verify

AI validates payload type, checks inventory, calculates optimal flight route and weather conditions.

03

Launch

Automated fulfillment center loads the cold-chain package and launches the VTOL drone within 2 minutes.

04

Deliver

Precision airdrop via biodegradable parachute within a 2-meter radius. Real-time tracking throughout.
